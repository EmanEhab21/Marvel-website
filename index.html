Avengers

The Avengers are a team of superheroes appearing in comic books published by Marvel Comics. They were created by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby and first appeared in "The Avengers" #1 in 1963. The original team consisted of Iron Man, Thor, Hulk, Ant-Man, and Wasp, with Captain America joining soon after. The team is known for its motto, "Earth's Mightiest Heroes," and their mission to combat threats too powerful for any single hero to handle.

Avengers Characters
